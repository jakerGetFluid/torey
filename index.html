About Me

Hello! My name is Torey. At this point in life I consider myself to be a community outreach specialist with a focus on nonprofit work and education. I lived in the beautiful state of Vermont from birth until May of 2015, when I moved to Salt Lake City, Utah. I moved away to quench my thirst for learning about other cultures and communities. I began my journey in Salt Lake City by becoming a Volunteer in Service to America (VISTA) through AmeriCorps at a nonprofit at Salt Lake Community College. After earning my bachelor’s degree from the University of Vermont, this including a study abroad experience for three and a half months in Oaxaca, Mexico. After college, I made a commitment to myself to be a life-long learner and to find work that helps solve the problems produced by globalization. I believe that both education and nonprofit work are key in producing positive changes, and I would like to participate.
I encourage you to look through my resume and published paper below. Please don’t hesitate to reach out to me.
